Quantum Entanglement in Neural Network States

by Sarah J. Thompson, Michael R. Chen, David A. Miller
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
PM Rating
Published 2023 • 26 Citations

Abstract

This paper explores the intersection of quantum mechanics and artificial neural networks, specifically investigating how quantum entanglement manifests in neural network states. We present a novel framework for understanding the relationship between quantum information and deep learning architectures, demonstrating that certain neural network configurations naturally encode quantum-like correlations. Our findings suggest new approaches for quantum-inspired machine learning algorithms and potential applications in quantum computing.
